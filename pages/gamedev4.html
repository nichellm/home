Journey Within 2: Maze of Doom Game Design

Game Story

This first person shooter is a continuation of the previous platformer where the player is lowered underground from the previous winning platform. Initially being on top of the mountain the elevator transports the player to an underground to maze filled with more malicious robots and crumbling hallway that they must navigate to totally leave the mountain. A retro gun is the first thing the player finds glowing before they enter the maze, but within there are other collectibles and health packs. One of the robots had a malfunction and is actually good, so if they player encounters this rare case of a potential friend it could increase his points as well.

Characters

  • Obbi- A “vintage” doll bot programmed to love humans, his journey starts by finding his friends and discovering what happened to humans.
  • Teddy- Another Vintage doll bot in the form of a teddy bear that will help counsel Obbi on the other levels
  • Haz Bots- Enemies that try to dismantle Obbi and his allies to mold them after the Without Humans Robot Initiative
